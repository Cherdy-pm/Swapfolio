# Swapfolio

## What is Swapfolio?

Swapfolio is a cutting-edge feature layer that allows retail investors to **swap one stock directly for another stock**â€”in a single, immediate transaction.

Instead of the tedious, two-step process (Sell Asset A for cash, then wait, then Buy Asset B), Swapfolio uses a single, **atomic exchange** (barter) mechanism. This process is already legal and exists institutionally, but Swapfolio makes it accessible to the everyday investor.

## Why Does It Matter? (The Problem We Solve)

In traditional trading, the "Sell first, then Buy" process creates two major pains, as identified by the illiquidity challenges in markets like the NGX:

1.  **Tax Friction:** Selling a stock immediately "realizes" your profit, triggering an immediate tax event (capital gains). This often makes investors hesitant to rebalance their portfolios.
2.  **Execution Inertia & Illiquidity:** When a highly illiquid stock (like a "Big-cap stock" with no immediate buyer) needs to be exchanged, the conventional two-step trade fails. Swapfolio offers a direct, guaranteed path.

**Swapfolio's Value:** We eliminate this friction. By executing an atomic, non-cash swap, we offer a tax-optimized transaction that is simpler, faster, and reduces your immediate tax liability (the **Tax Delta**).

## Who Is Swapfolio For?

* **Active Retail Investors:** People who frequently rebalance their portfolio and are looking to optimize their transactions.
* **Tax-Conscious Traders:** Investors with significant unrealized gains who want to manage their tax exposure more efficiently.
* **Users of Sub-Brokerage Platforms:** We are designed to integrate seamlessly as a premium feature into existing mobile trading apps (e.g., Bamboo).

## Key Features

| Feature | Investor Benefit |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Atomic Swap Button** | Executes the entire exchange in one click, removing multi-step complexity. |
| **Real-Time Ratio Estimator** | Instantly shows the precise number of shares you will receive based on current market price parity. |
